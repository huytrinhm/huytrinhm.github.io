👋 Hi there!

I'm Huy - a tech-enthusiast living in Vietnam 🇻🇳.


It was on my 8th birthday that I had my first laptop, and since then I have always been fascinated by the power of computers and technology. I tried to learn my first "programming language": Scratch, and luckily won a prize in DXC Codes gamemaking challenge 😅!


With a curious mind and an urge to develop, I soon realised that Computer Science is my biggest passion. In 2020, I got into the Class of Informatics at VNU-HCM High School for the Gifted. Here, I was introduced to the fundamental foundation of CS: Data Structures and Algorithms, Web Development, Software Development...


To put the tools and knowledge that I learned at school into practice, I have worked on many personal projects covering various topics: websites, games, chatbots, browser extensions and even artificial intelligence.
